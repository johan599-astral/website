How it works

We start by auditing your current workflows — finding every manual step that's costing your team time. Then we design and deploy automation sequences using best-in-class tools like n8n, Make (formerly Integromat), and Zapier to connect your sales channels, banking feeds, payroll, and accounting software.

The result: your books are updated automatically throughout the month, not just at month-end. You get a live view of your finances, any time you need it.
