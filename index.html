About our company

Built by accountants, powered by automation

After decades of experience in corporate accounting and consulting, our team has seen it all. In today's business environment, small to medium sized businesses are being significantly under served. Our goal is to level the playing field and help these local companies grow.

Based in Atlanta, Georgia and with teammates across the US, we are aiming at becoming the leader in next-generation bookkeeping and accounting consulting. Our approach utilizes our vast experience, along with emerging automation technology to deliver results for our clients.

No project is too big or small for us. Whether you are a local coffee shop struggling with integrating your PoS into an accounting system, or a technology company that just reached $1M in ARR — you will find that our company can add tremendous value!
